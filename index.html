I am a Ph.D student at the School of Computing, National University of Singapore (NUS), advised by Prof. Jin-Song Dong. Previously, I received M.Eng degree from Tsinghua University and B.Eng degree from Hohai University. I was a research intern at Ping An Technology, working on the robustness of face anti-spoofing. I was also an intern developer for MindSpore at Huawei. My research directions mainly include AI security, machine learning and wireless sensor networks.
